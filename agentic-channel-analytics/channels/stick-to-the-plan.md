## Identity

- Niche: **longevidad / healthspan / hábitos** — vivir más y mejor: sueño, ayuno,

## The name (triple pun — keep the niche tight to make it land)
1. **Stick** = palito → the stickman visual style (already the pipeline's style).
2. **Stick to the plan** = constancia/disciplina → exactly what longevity & habits are about.
3. The stickman **is the viewer** (everyman), more relatable than a mascot.
> "Cualquier tema" is a PHASE-2 option, not launch. Niche focus = faster algo classification.

## L1 — The moat: the recurring character (replaces the banana lexicon)
**Stick** — the protagonist of every video:
- **doodle stickman but a bit MUSCULAR / good-looking** (locked variant "B", 2026-06-24),
  **pelo largo, cinta en el pelo, gafas rojas**. Same design every time.
- Visual reference: `assets/stick-reference.png` · Higgsfield job_id `673502ef-9bf9-4db4-8d9a-eb5d949fb6ee`
  (pass this id as a reference image when generating frames to keep him consistent).
- Style: thick black marker doodle lines on white; red glasses are the only color accent.
- Premise: Stick intenta *cumplir el plan* (sus hábitos) y casi siempre lo sabotea su
  pereza/impulso. Cualquier tema de salud cuelga de su historia.
- He appears in **every thumbnail** → instant brand recognition in the feed.
- Each video is its OWN story (a bad night, a 30-day challenge, a day at the office…),
  not a fixed narrative. The constant is the character + the voice + the metaphor toolkit.

## SEO notes
- Title pattern: **SEO-first** keyword title, sin sufijo gimmick. La marca la lleva
  Stick en la miniatura. Front-load the longevity keyword.
- Description: hook line (125 first chars) + niche keywords + chapters + disclaimer
  ("no es consejo médico") + chained link + 3–5 hashtags.
- Thumbnail: **Stick siempre presente** (pelo largo, cinta, gafas rojas), 1 símbolo, ≤3
  words, legible en móvil, identidad consistente. Never an auto-frame.
- **Typography (locked 2026-06-24):** whenever text appears (thumbnails, on-screen
  emphasis words) it must be **bold cartoon 3D, black & white** — thick chunky 3D
  extruded letters, high contrast, no color. The red is reserved for Stick's glasses.
- Category: Educación. Idioma: español. Playlist por pilar.

## CRITICAL — YMYL + AI voice risk
Salud = YMYL; YouTube 2026 exige "humano real" para monetizar. Faceless + voz IA en
salud = alto riesgo. Mitigar: citar fuentes, sin promesas de cura, disclaimer en cada
vídeo, considerar voz humana real, esfuerzo original visible.
